CEO Aryan Sharma Profile

Flagship Product | Multaan's Multani Mitti

Multani Mitti, also known as Fuller's Earth, is a natural clay that has been used for centuries in skincare, haircare, and even for medicinal purposes. It's called Multani Mitti in Hindi, and it's often recognized for its healing and cleansing properties. Here are some important aspects of Multani Mitti

Skin Cleansing: It has excellent cleansing properties, helping to remove dirt, excess oil, and impurities from the skin. It makes an effective face pack for those with oily skin. Acne Treatment: Multani Mitti absorbs excess oil from the skin, which can be a major cause of acne. It has a cooling effect and can help reduce inflammation. Improves Skin Texture: Regular use of Multani Mitti helps improve skin tone and texture. It can reduce blemishes, dark spots, and even out complexion. Removes Dead Skin Cells: It acts as a gentle exfoliator, removing dead skin cells, and making the skin feel soft and smooth. Hydrates Skin: Multani Mitti can help restore moisture to the skin, leaving it hydrated and glowing, especially when mixed with other moisturizing ingredients like honey or rose water. Hair Care: It can be applied to the scalp to absorb excess oil and treat dandruff, promoting healthier hair. Soothes Sunburn: Due to its cooling properties, Multani Mitti can also be used to treat sunburns and reduce redness or irritation caused by the sun. How to Use Multani Mitti: Face Pack: Mix Multani Mitti with water, rose water, or other natural ingredients like honey or lemon juice. Apply the paste to your face and leave it for 10–15 minutes before rinsing off with lukewarm water. Hair Mask: Mix Multani Mitti with yogurt or aloe vera gel and apply it to the scalp and hair. Leave it on for about 20–30 minutes before rinsing it off.
